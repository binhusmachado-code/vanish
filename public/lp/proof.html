FAQ

Common questions

Every removal generates a confirmation record — either an email from the broker or a verified re-scan showing your listing no longer appears. Both are stored in your dashboard with timestamps. You can re-scan any broker at any time to verify your data is still gone.
Data brokers frequently re-acquire data from public records. VANISH runs quarterly re-scans on all covered brokers and automatically resubmits removal requests if you reappear. This is included in your subscription at no extra cost.
Most brokers process removals within 72 hours to 30 days depending on their internal policies. The majority of our customers see confirmed removals within the first week, with 90%+ complete by day 45. Your dashboard shows real-time progress throughout.
We currently cover 484 data brokers, people-search sites, and marketing data aggregators — including BeenVerified, Spokeo, Intelius, Whitepages, MyLife, PeopleFinder, TruthFinder, and hundreds more. We add new brokers monthly as we identify them.
We use the minimum information necessary to submit removal requests — typically just your name, city, and state. Your information is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your data to third parties, and we never use your information for advertising. You can delete your VANISH account at any time.
